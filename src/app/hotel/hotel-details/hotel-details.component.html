Description

Set on 3600 acres and nestled along Lake Las Vegas, The Westin Lake Las Vegas Resort & Spa offers comfortable, spacious guest rooms, access to world class golf courses and a variety of on-site dining options. The Las Vegas Strip is 30 minutes’ drive away. Guest rooms at The Westin Lake Las Vegas Resort & Spa boast flat-screen TVs.

Guest rooms at The Westin Lake Las Vegas Resort & Spa boast flat-screen TVs. Guests can relax at the end of the day with luxurious Westin Heavenly Bath toiletries and cozy bathrobes. After a restful night of sleep, guests can wake up with a free cup of in-room coffee.

The Westin Lake Las Vegas offers a variety of dining options. Marssa Steak & Sushi serves world-class Japanese and Asian-fusion cuisine, while Rick's Cafe serves American cuisine with Mediterranean influences. The poolside Sandsa Bar & Grill offer exotic cocktails and delicious menu items. After dinner, visitors can have a signature cocktail in the hotel's Arabesque Lounge.
